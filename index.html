FILM FESTIVAL | 2017
Catharsis is the national annual film festival of BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus. Catharsis started off in 2014 as an intra college film festival, Catharsis in a very short span of time has grew to become one of the most admired film festivals in India. With participation from more than 50 colleges from all over India catharsis has been successful in hosting events that appeal to the masses. The fest also hosts talks and pro-shows with celebrities of national and international renown appearing and/or performing the fest. This year we have come up with new events that seek to cherish the cultural diversity of our country.
Kaleidoscope

India's Biggest Short Film Competition is here! CATHARSIS FILM FESTIVAL, BITS PILANI-Hyderabad as a part of Pearl'17, proudly presents Kaleidoscope, One of the biggest Short Film competitions of the year. With Prizes, worth Rs 75k in cash, this promises to be a mouthwatering prospect for every aspiring Film Maker. To Participate, submit your entries to:- movieclub@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.in Your movie can be in any language, but it is compulsory to submit English subtitles along with the movie. The last date for submission is 25th February, 2017. Attached in the link below are the complete rules and regulations for the competitions, kindly go through them before submitting your entries.

VividH

WE, at BITS PILANI Hyderabad Campus, take pride in being a citizen of India and we have come up with our own way to celebrate it. India is a country Full of Diversity, yet we stand united. Because the diversity only makes the country More Beautiful and Vivid. In the same Spirit, Catharsis Film Festival as a part of Pearl'17, Launches vividH, a one of its Kind Cultural Fair, where you send in Video Entries (Short films, Mobile made clips, Documentaries), Photo Stories or Motion Posters with a 50 word write up, which Describes the Beauty in the diversity of the traditions and culture in your locality and how it Makes India Incredible. It can be about Ganga, or any local festival or traditional dance form or about the way of living and Habits of the people in your locality. With Prize Money worth Rs 30,000, the selected entries will be awarded and displayed during Catharsis and Pearl, Our annual Cultural Festival from March 17th to 19th. You can be from any part of the country, of any age group. All we are looking for is that sense of Nationalism and Pride in you, to show the world the different colors that make India Vivid. Send In your entries or queries to movieclub@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.in or contact us at +91 9553339766
